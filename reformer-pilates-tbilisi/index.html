Reformer Pilates in a space that feels like yours.

Her House is a luxury women's wellness club and reformer Pilates studio in Vera, Tbilisi. Our small-group classes combine strength, control, breath, and connection in a warm boutique setting.

What is reformer Pilates?

Reformer Pilates uses a spring-based machine to support controlled movement, strength, posture, mobility, and body awareness. It can feel gentle and challenging at the same time.

Who is it for?

Her House welcomes women who are new to Pilates, returning to movement, or looking for a refined weekly practice in Tbilisi.

Why Her House?

The studio combines reformer Pilates with a women-only community, boutique design, post-class connection, and a premium but approachable wellness-club feeling.

Where is the studio?

Her House is located in Vera, one of Tbilisi's most central and walkable neighborhoods.
