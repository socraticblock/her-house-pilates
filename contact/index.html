How to find us

The studio is on Zakaria Paliashvili Street, in the heart of Vera. Look for the Her House signage at street level. Ring the bell marked Her House at the entrance, and our team will come down to welcome you.

Closest public transport: Rustaveli metro is a 7-minute walk. Street parking is available on Paliashvili and the surrounding blocks.
