Beginner Pilates Tbilisi

First time? You are exactly where you need to be.

Whether you are new to Pilates or already love reformer classes, our instructors guide you through the movement and help you feel comfortable from the moment you arrive.

Is it beginner-friendly?

Yes. Beginners are welcome, and classes are taught with calm, clear guidance.

What should I wear?

Comfortable fitted movement clothes. Grip socks may be requested for safety and comfort.

When should I arrive?

Please arrive 10 minutes early so we can welcome you and help you settle in.

Can I come with a friend?

Yes, if spaces are available. Each person should have a reserved place.

Pregnant or injured?

Please message the studio before booking so the team can advise safely.

How does booking work?

Choose a class, leave your name and WhatsApp number, and your place is reserved.
