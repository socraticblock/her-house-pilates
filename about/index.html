About Her House

A place for movement, friendship, and feeling at home in your body.

Her House is more than a Pilates studio. It is a feminine, premium, social wellness space built around reformer Pilates, connection, beauty, and belonging.

Her House was created as a luxury wellness club for women in Vera, Tbilisi - a place to move with intention and return to yourself.

Beautiful space. Good energy. Amazing women. The studio is designed to feel premium, warm, and safe.

The Community

Post-class chats, rooftop sunsets, and women supporting each other are part of what makes Her House feel like home.
